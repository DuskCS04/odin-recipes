Bulgogi Recipe

Bulgogi is a dish that my dad made when I was younger since my family is Mormon and he personally went on a mission to South Korea. this gave him the love for Korean food and therefore passed it onto me for his love of asian food in his life. Bulgogi is a simple beef marinated in a sweet and savory marinade dish that you put onto rice. This has been such a wonderful meal that my dad makes and will always be special in my heart.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • 2 ½ tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, green onion, sugar, garlic, sesame seeds, sesame oil, and pepper together in a bowl.
  3. Place flank steak slices in a shallow dish. Pour marinade over top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Quickly grill flank steak slices on the preheated grill until slightly charred and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
